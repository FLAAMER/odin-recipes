Chicken Wing Dip

Description

This chicken wing dip tastes just like hot chicken wings, but without the mess. It is perfect for football and tailgating season. I take this whenever I have to bring a dish and am always asked for the recipe.

Serve with tortilla chips.

Ingredients

  • 2(8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 3/4 cup hot pepper sauce
  • 2 cups diced cooked chicken
  • 1 cup Ranch-style salad dressing
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Mix cream cheese and hot pepper sauce together in a medium bowl until well blended; stir in chicken, dressing, and Cheddar cheese. Spread into a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until bubbly, about 30 minutes. If oil collects on the top, dab it off using a paper towel.
